Warp Stone
Cost  3,200
Tier 3
Active 16
Teleport straight ahead, gaining Bullet Resist.
11m Teleport Range
+40% Bullet Resist (Conditional)
6s Buff Duration


Codename: upgrade_warp_stone

Warp Stone is a Tier 3 Vitality Item. Its active effect teleports the user directly ahead, granting them bonus Bullet Resist for a few seconds.

It can be bought from the Shop for  3,200.

Update history[edit]

Main article: Update history
Update Changes

May 8, 2025

  • Moved from Weapon category to Vitality
  • Price increased from 3,000 to 3,200
  • No longer grants +20% Weapon Damage or +8 Spirit Power
  • Conditional Bullet Resist increased from +30% to +40%
  • Buff duration increased from 5s to 6s

April 17, 2025

  • Fixed   Phantom Strike and   Warp Stone working through   Binding Word[sic] capture

September 26, 2024

  • Casting while on the ground will no longer stop the player in place, will try to move forward along the ground instead.
  • Fixed cases of   Warp Stone getting caught on geometry.
